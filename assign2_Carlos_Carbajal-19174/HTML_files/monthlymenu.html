The Amazing Cheeseburger

Our Amazing Cheeseburger has been a staple and a fan favorite at our cafeteria. First timers can always count on a pleasant experience with our fine cheddar and 95% lean beef patties, smoked for a great tasting burger. *comes with a side of fries topped with sea salt and a side of onion rings.

Farmer Charmer

The Farmer Charmer comes with a butter glazed bun topped with sesame seeds, grilled chicken, 3 bacon strips, and 2 eggs any style. All of these ingredients come from our local farms. *comes with a side of potato wedges and guacamole dip.

The Joe

The Joe comes with a butter glazed bun and 95% lean ground beef. Our special in house sloppy joe sauce makes all the difference, here. *comes with a side of coleslaw and mashed potatoes.

Fettuccine Alfredo $12.99

Our fettuccine alfredo comes with a family secret homemade alfredo sauce that has won numerous awards. *comes with a side of mashed potatoes and broccoli.

Beef Lasagna $11.99

Our beef lasagna is topped with feta and mozzarella cheese. *comes with a asparagus and corn.

Spaghetti $12.99

Our spaghetti comes with garlic and basil infused tomato sauce. *comes with corn and a loaded baked potato.

Olive and Artichoke Pizza $14.99

Our signature vegan dish is a pizza with olives and artichoke. Toppings can be swapped. *comes with breadsticks.

Vegetable Salad $9.50

This vegetable salad includes avocados, tomatoes, spinash, olives, and croutons. *comes with your choice of dressing.

Tofu Rice with Broccoli $11.79

This fan favorite tofu dish comes with delicious rice and steamed broccoli. *comes with your choice of dip.

Fried Chicken Tenders with Waffles $13.99

Our fried chicken tenders and waffles are our most popular breakfast dish for a reason. Top them off with our signature style Honestly Amazing syrup. *comes with a side of buttered toast.

4 Egg Omelette $10.99

Pick your own spices and add any additional protein ($1.50 extra) to your 4 egg any way you want it omelette. *comes with a side of hash browns and buttered toast.

Sausage Burrito $8.99

Our sausage burritos are wrapped in keto friendly tortillas with 3 eggs, tomatoes, and green peppers. *comes with a side of hashbrowns and buttered toast.
